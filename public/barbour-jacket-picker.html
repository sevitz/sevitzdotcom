Showerproof

Designed to handle light rain and brief downpours. These jackets use treated cotton or polyamide fabrics that repel water on the surface, keeping you dry in passing showers. Lighter, more breathable, and typically softer in feel. Ideal for everyday wear when you want protection without the bulk, but not suited to prolonged heavy rain.

Waterproof

Built for sustained rain and heavier weather. These jackets use sealed seams and membranes or coatings that prevent water from penetrating, even during extended exposure. Slightly more structured and protective, they are the better choice for longer walks, outdoor days, or unpredictable weather where staying completely dry matters.
